1. Leap Year Checker

Problem:

Write a method that takes a year and checks whether it’s a leap year or not.

Logic:

A year is considered a leap year if:

  • It is divisible by 4,
  • But not divisible by 100,
  • Unless it is also divisible by 400
Examples:

Input Output Reason
2000 Leap Year Divisible by 400
1900 Not a Leap Year Divisible by 100 but not 400
2024 Leap Year Divisible by 4 and not divisible by 100
2023 Not a Leap Year Not divisible by 4

2. Character Type Checker

Problem:

Accept a character input and check if it is:

  • An Uppercase Letter
  • A Lowercase Letter
  • A Digit
  • A Special Character
Logic:

Use ASCII value ranges::

  • 'A'-'Z' → Uppercaser
  • 'a'-'z' → Lowercase
  • '0'-'9' → Digit
  • Anything else → Special character
Examples:

Input Output
'A' Uppercase Letter
'b' Lowercase Letter
'7' Digit
'#' Special Character

3. Simple Calculator (Switch Version)

Problem:

Create a method calculate(int a, int b, char op) to perform:

  • Addition +
  • Subtraction -
  • Multiplication *
  • Division /
  • Modulo %
Logic:

Use a switch(op) statement to handle operation choices.

Examples:

Input Output
10, 3, '*' 30
10, 5, '+' 15
20, 4, '/' 5
15, 4, '%' 3
20, 12, '-' 8

5. Triangle Type Checker

Problem:

Accept 3 side lengths of a triangle and determine its type:

  • Equilateral: all sides equal
  • Isosceles: any two sides equal
  • Scalene: all sides different
Examples:

Input Output
5, 5, 5 Equilateral triangle
5, 5, 7 Isosceles triangle
4, 5, 6 Scalene triangle
